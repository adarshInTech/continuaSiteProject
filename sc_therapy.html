SC Therapy


Empowering Special Needs Children through Online Special Education

Specially-abled children often face challenges in daily activities and selective physical movements. Physiotherapy plays a crucial role in addressing these challenges by strengthening their muscles, improving coordination, enhancing flexibility, and correcting posture. Our online therapy platform offers comprehensive home physiotherapy programs designed specifically for specially-abled kids, allowing them to experience the benefits of physiotherapy from the comfort of their own homes.

Partnering with a Paediatric Physiotherapist


Our team of skilled paediatric physiotherapists works closely with families to identify motor skill deficiencies and assess any underlying chronic conditions before commencing therapy. With their expertise and specialized training, our physiotherapists create customized treatment plans tailored to meet each child's unique needs.

What to Expect from Online Home Physiotherapy


Our online home physiotherapy programs involve a wide range of physical activities and exercises, each serving a specific purpose to address the child's mobility issues.

Children who face challenges in communicating at an age-appropriate level can benefit greatly from speech therapy. Parents should be aware of the following signs that may indicate the need for intervention:

Passive Streching

Gentle stretching exercises to improve flexibility and range of motion.

Manual Streching

Targeted stretching techniques administered by the physiotherapist to enhance muscle flexibility.

Aerobics

Fun and engaging aerobic exercises to promote cardiovascular fitness and overall well-being.

Weight Bearing

Activities that involve bearing weight on different body parts to strengthen muscles and improve balance.

Breating Exercises

Techniques to enhance respiratory function and promote efficient breathing patterns.

Splinting

Utilization of splints or orthotic devices to support and align body parts for optimal movement and posture.

Agility Training

Exercises focused on improving agility, coordination, and motor skills.

Endurance and Strength Exercises

Activities that enhance endurance and strength to improve overall physical function.

Scientifically Proven Treatment Approaches

All the techniques employed in our physiotherapy programs are backed by scientific evidence and proven to be effective. We embrace three standard treatment approaches:

Active Therapy

Engaging the child in purposeful physical activities to promote active participation and skill development.

Passive Therapy

Utilizing manual techniques and assisted movements to address muscle tightness and improve range of motion.

Home Exercise Program

Providing families with personalized home exercise programs that complement the therapy sessions, ensuring continued progress and improvement.
