Who Can Benefit from Online Speech Therapy?


Children who face challenges in communicating at an age-appropriate level can benefit greatly from speech therapy. Parents should be aware of the following signs that may indicate the need for intervention:

Speech Disorder

This includes stammering, misarticulation, and fluency issues that affect the clarity of speech.

Voice Disorder

Problems with pitch, loudness, and tone that make speech unclear to listeners.

Language Disorders

Difficulties in language comprehension, expression, and cognitive-communication.

Swallowing Difficulties

Structural abnormalities in facial muscles, throat, or neck that can affect speech and swallowing.
